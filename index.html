A GRAVEYARD
OF POSSIBILITIES

        


        

          

            
A massive ghost ship hangs in low orbit over a lost colony on Tau Ceti IV. The 30,000 souls who called this place home have disappeared without a trace. Strange signals hint at mysterious artifacts, long-dormant AI, and troves of untold riches.

            
The year is 2893 — ninety-nine years after the UESC Marathon went dark. Now the perimeter is open, and Runners — bio-cybernetic mercenaries in expendable synthetic shells — drop planetside to strip New Cascadia for salvage, data, and answers.

            
You are one of them. Whatever erased the colony is still down there.
Death hangs above.
